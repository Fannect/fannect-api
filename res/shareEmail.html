What is Fannect?

Fannect is a mobile sports app that seeks to answer one question: "Who has the best sports fans?"


Does your team have the best fans? Of course they do! But wait... how do you prove it? Fannect proves which teams have the best fans by quantifying what it means to be a sports fan. How? Great question.

At the core of the Fannect sports app are the Fannect Games. Each of our games measures a fan's passion, dedication, or knowledge.

Interact

In Fannect you can...

  • Compete for your team alongside fellow fans by playing Fannect Games

  • Measure your Passion, Dedication and Knowledge of your favorite teams

  • Compare your team's fans to your rivals fans... and many other comparisons

  • Ineract with other passionate sports fans
